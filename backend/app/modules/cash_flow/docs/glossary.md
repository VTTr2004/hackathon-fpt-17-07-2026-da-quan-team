# Glossary

- **Operating cash flow:** cash from principal revenue-producing activities and related operating payments.
- **Investing cash flow:** cash used for or received from long-term assets/investments.
- **Financing cash flow:** cash changing contributed equity or borrowings.
- **Gross burn:** average operating cash outflow per period.
- **Net burn:** positive amount by which average operating outflow exceeds operating inflow.
- **Runway:** available cash divided by positive net burn.
- **Reconciliation:** comparison of opening cash plus cash movements with reported ending cash.
- **Contribution margin ratio:** one minus variable-cost ratio.
- **Break-even revenue:** fixed cost divided by contribution margin ratio.
- **Net working capital:** accounts receivable plus inventory minus accounts payable.
- **DSO/DPO:** estimated collection/payment days using matching-period balances and flows.
- **Cash conversion cycle:** DSO plus inventory days minus DPO.
- **Autofill proposal:** document-derived candidate fact with stable ID, provenance, confidence and warnings; not yet confirmed.
- **Mapping plan:** AI or heuristic selection of sheet/header/columns and deterministic ingestion tools.
- **Canonical schema:** stable internal field names that isolate calculations from workbook-specific headers.
